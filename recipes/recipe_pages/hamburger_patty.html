The Perfect Patty

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Ground Chuck
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Crushed Red Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Combine ingredients in a large mixing bowl and split into even halfs

  2. Shape each half into a ball and press down in the middle while slowly working your way out to form patties. Next, create an indention 1/4 inch deep with two fingers in the middle of the pattie.

  3. Heat a heavy bottomed skillet on high. Once hot, place patties in pan on one side for 3 minutes before flipping for another 3 minutes.

  4. Add a half stick of butter to the pan and reduce heat to medium low. With a spoon, baste the patties and let sit until desired temprature is reached.
